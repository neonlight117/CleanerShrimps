Our Mission

What is Cleaner Shrimps?

Cleaner Shrimps is a social media platform creatd to help bring people together for one simple purpose: picking up litter. All across the country and around the world, many concerned individuals and environmental organizations have taken it upon themselves to do something about the abundant amounts of litter out loose in our environment. Cleaner Shrimps gives users a way to meet likeminded neighbors, organize cleanup events, track their progress, find cleanup sites, recommend sites to others, contribute to scientific research.

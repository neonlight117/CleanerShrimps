About Us


Who We Are

Cleaner Shrimps was founded by two sisters, Kristen and Dana Mastropole. Their passion for environmental justice and their love of the ocean led Kristen into the field of marine biology and Dana into the field of physical oceanography.

The story of Cleaner Shrimps is a familiar one. While living abroad, Kristen got a first-hand look at the devastating effects our throwaway culture had on the ocean, and realized how much trouble we would be if we ignored the reality of what was going on. On a trip to Bali, during a time the local residents have aptly nicknamed "Garbage Season", she encountered a sight that she had only before seen in pictures - a beach so littered with garbage that it was impossible to put a foot down without stepping on plastic. From that moment on, she wanted to do something to help combat the plastic pollution crisis. Upon returning home to Hong Kong, she began volunteering with local beach cleanup groups and considered her role in creating plastic waste. When she moved back home to the US, she shared her vision of Cleaner Shrimps with her sister, Dana, who enthusiastically put those ideas into action.
